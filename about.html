Uzmaan Ghouse began his career as a hotelier organizing events at the Ceylon Continental Hotel and the Cinnamon Grand Colombo. At the age of 23 he founded Eventz N Trendz with Iyanthie de Silva in 2009.

Uzmaan was interviewed by "BBC News" in April 2011. BBC News telecasted a wedding program, "Extreme World - Weddings" two weeks before the ROYAL wedding of Prince William & Kate Middleton.

Since 2021, Uzmaan has been carrying on as the Owner and CEO of Eventz N Trendz.

The company organized the weddings of Celebrities like Santhush Weeraman, Ajantha Mendis, Rangana Herath, Nadini Premadasa, Farveez Maharoof & Lahiru Perera. They also organized the weddings of the sons' of Former Prime Minister D.M.Jayaratne and Former President Maithripala Sirisena.

From the CEO

“Eventz N Trendz have had the honor and privilege to have worked with over hundreds of individuals to make their visions come to life. We are truly blessed to have been such an integral part of so many corporate occasions and wedding celebrations. Working so closely with such diverse personalities and cultures over the years has been priceless. We love working hard to fulfill their visions and dreams of their Perfect Day”
